At what stage?Graduate student (the actual researcher): Experiment in a lab or simulation in a computer or mathematical formulation. Then depending on the stage and funding of the program, side quests like, teaching assistance, advanced classes and qualifying exams. Later stages peppered occasionally with travel.Post-doc (extension of grad student): Same as grad student but no teaching, classes or qualifiers. Some supervisory duties of grad students.Professors (from adjuncts to department heads): Grand writing, research group meetings, other meetings, classroom teaching, administrative paper pushing peppered with travel.
