The most difficult aspect of an entrepreneur's day is trying to figure out what NOT to do. My days at Expertcity (creator of GoToMyPC and GoToMeeting, sold to Citrix) where a non-stop tennis match in which I played against a couple hundred people.My job was to keep most of the two hundred+ tennis balls in play, will simultaneously trying to win some points (close deals), return volley others (keep an ongoing dialog going) and allow still others to hit the wall behind me (issues that didn't warrant my immediate attention).Most successful entrepreneurs want to hit every ball back, but you must force yourself to allow some to fly on by...
